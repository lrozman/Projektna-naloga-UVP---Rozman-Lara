Synopsis

Wakamatsu Masato lives with his step-sister, Miyuki, whom he does not have blood relationship with. Their father is always working oversea and left the two of them living by themselves in Toukyou. Miyuki is cute, nice, sensitive and very popular among guys around Masato. Masato has a girlfriend at school whose name is Kajima Miyuki, who is pretty and gentle, the ideal dream of every highschool boy.

this is my first review so take it easy ^_^ , Miyuki is a romance slice of life anime the talk about a guy called Masato who live alone by himself,and his father and his little sister that called Miyuki are living in Canada, and he didn't meet his sister for 6 years , one day his father decided to send his sister back home in Japan to take care of him, then he figured out that his sister is adopted. and he love a girl in his class that also called Miyuki. so you will see kind of love triangle between the main ... protagonist and the two Miyukis, the show focus more on comedy and i have to say it is funny most of the time, and it will put the protagonist choosing between the two Miyukis.

story 7\10
the storyline was fine as the characters developed a bit each episode so you will feel the charecters getting closer to each other and understanding each other feelings,however some of the side characters didn't develop at all, and some of them are really annoying and always ruining the moment and that kind of pissed me off. in the other hand there are some really worm moment between the main protagonist and his little sister, that show the strong relationship they have,and how this affect his relationship with the other Miyuki that is his a classmate.

Animation 5\10
because the anime is really old, animations is pretty bad. however that didn't affect my enjoyment while watching that much, it really depends on the person himself, if you ok with old animation style then you will not face that much trouble watching this anime.

Sound 7/10
the voice acting is surprisingly good special the littel sister ( imoto ) voice acting she's a pretty cute , and the music is pretty good as well its old style but do suit the moments, my only complaint that the anime is a pretty old so you feel kind of noise in the background and the sound effects most of them are pretty bad as well.

Character 8/10
let's put it that way the main three characters of the serious are really good,and you will feel really connected to them. however the side characters are average one of them is it pretty funny and always add to the anime, but most of them are just here to give some comedy here and there and there comedy will get old really fast.

Enjoyment 7/10
this anime is really enjoyable if you into a clumsy romance slice of life that have a good triangle between the characters, and good characters development. most of the comedy is funny as well, and you will feel really connected to the main characters, if old animation is not a problem for you I say go ahead and watch it.

Recommended
Preliminary (15/37 eps)
The Adachi Mitsuru nostalgia just hits different with its Adachi nostalgia, alongside with its banger OP. I am surprised this anime is rated in the low 6's consisting of about 600 Users. Personally I am fond of this and it may be due to my biased appreciation to the old anime art style, in such, it is also created by my favourite mangaka Adachi Mitsuru.

Old art style may not be everyone's preferred anime, and the anime does have many flaws that can be seen in different perspectives. An example includes slow-pacing and the weird character choices with annoying/disturbing personalities, however I believe these flaws ... make up the anime to be good (personal preference)

I love how each series Adachi Mitsuru makes, the Adachi universe is linked in each series. For example, you are most likely to see characters or other baseball schools from different series appear. Like in this anime (ep15) You see the team "Meisei" which is related to the anime Mix:Meisei and Touch. The Adachi universe is endless, and its amazing where he references atleast 1 thing from each series of his favourite sports (baseball, boxing or both).

Overall the series great, yet so underrated. Story is decent (7/10), sound is very good (9/10) such as; the amazing OP and the voice acting/ good characters (some can be quite annoying due to their repetitive trait), and was very enjoyable to relax and watch this anime.

In summary, the art style is veery subjective as the preference of the watcher may vary, and personally I really like this art style. Basically Its a really great anime and I understand why people may believe this deserves a low score, however it is all based on personal preference, as I rate this about 9/10.

ok let's talk about Miyuki, This isn't the first time I've watched this type of story, i have seen the same story with the same incomplete ending and the same drama before, and it was written by the same writer.

story - after a brief description, i find its a story between three-character mc , his sister (not blood related) and mc's girlfriend but mainly focus on mc and his sister .but the show was not like that.

the first few episodes will make you laugh, then you will wait for some character's development and romance between then , but they will show you every episode on repeat. In the show, the main character (MC) has a girlfriend who blames him for everything and slaps him every time. At the end of each episode, she realizes it wasn't his mistake and they will resolve their matter in the end of every episode ( This pattern repeats from the first episode to the end of the show.)

there is a sister who has feelings for the MC but never shows them throughout the entire show. The same goes for the MC, as their relationship gets no development, and the show ends without any resolution, which is frustrating.

You will also encounter annoying side characters in the show who try to get close to the sister by bullying the MC, while she pretends like nothing happened.

in the end Miyuki is a show which has no proper story no romance no development just a crap which feels like on repeat ..
